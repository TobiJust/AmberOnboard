uint8_t OpCurveDetection::initialize ( )

Initialisation of LookUp tables.

This function creates a LookUp table which assigns a distance in meter from the front of a car to the corresponding line number on the image.

the formula is: linenumber = (numberOfLines/2)(1 + cameraHeight/((distance + camOffset)*tan(vAngleOfView/2)))

Formula is calculated assuming the street is even and the horizon line lies exacly in the midle of the image

vAngleOfView - vertical angle of view of the camera camHeight - distance from camera to the street camOffset - distance from camera to the fromt of the car distance - distance from the front of the car to some point in meters

Parameters
imgSource image
